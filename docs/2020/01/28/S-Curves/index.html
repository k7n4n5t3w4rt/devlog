S-Curves

نُشر في 2020-01-28 عُدل في 2020-08-15

S-Curves are a maths thing. Their application in business strategy comes from Charles Handy

Dave Snowden linked them with Moore’s Crossing The Chasm and, [gradually]](http://cognitive-edge.com/blog/taken-at-the-flood/) found his way to apex preditor theory

Simon Wardley also references s-curves, which is particularly interesting in terms of his ideas on the natural evolution of things from genesis, through custom and product, to commodity.
